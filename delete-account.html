Delete Your Blips Account

This page explains how to delete your account and associated data in the Blips app, published by Blips Digital Inc.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Blips app and sign in.
  2. Go to your Profile and open Settings.
  3. Under Account Actions, tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Can't access the app?

If you can no longer sign in, email support@blipsdigital.com from the email address associated with your account and request deletion. We will verify ownership and process the request.

What is deleted

  • Your profile (username, display name, photo, bio)
  • All blips (videos), photos, comments, replies, and voice messages you created
  • Your followers/following relationships and in-app messages
  • Your saved and viewed history

Your account and content become invisible to other users immediately upon deletion.

What is kept, and for how long

After deletion, your data is permanently removed from active systems on a scheduled purge. We retain a minimal record (account ID and deletion timestamp) for moderation and fraud-prevention review for a reasonable period before final deletion, and we may retain specific records where required by law. Government-ID images submitted for verification (if any) are automatically deleted within 30 days of submission regardless of account status.

Deleting some data without deleting your account

You can delete individual pieces of content — such as a blip, comment, or message — at any time from inside the app, without deleting your account. Deleted content is hidden immediately and its files are permanently removed on a scheduled purge. See our Privacy Policy for full details on retention.
