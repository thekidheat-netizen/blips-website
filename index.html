A New Way to Share What's Around You

📍

Drop a Blip Anywhere

Record a short video and anchor it to the exact spot you took it. Friends and people nearby can discover it on the map and watch what you saw, right where you saw it.

🗺️

Real-World Discovery

Skip the algorithm. Walk into a neighborhood, scroll the map, and find what locals are sharing. Concert tonight, food truck line, sunset spot — the world is the feed.

👥

Direct, Friends, or Public

Every Blip lets you choose who sees it: a single person, your friends only, or anyone passing by. You control how private (or public) every moment is.

🪙

BlipCoins

Walk up to coins dropped on the map, watch the content, and earn rewards. Real-world treasure hunt built into the social layer.

🛡️

Privacy-First

On-device location processing, no contact list uploads (phone numbers are hashed locally), and screenshot protection on private content. Your moments stay yours.

📲

Built for iOS and Android

Native performance on both platforms. Designed for fast recording, instant uploads, and smooth map browsing.

How Blips Works

1

Open the map

Blips opens to a live map of your area. Existing Blips appear as pins where they were recorded.

2

Record a moment

Tap the camera, hold to record a short video, and choose who you want to share it with.

3

Drop it on the map

Your Blip anchors to the exact GPS coordinates you recorded at. People nearby (or your friends anywhere) can find and watch it.
