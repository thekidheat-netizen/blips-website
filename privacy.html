Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Blips, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile information
  • Content: Videos, photos, and other content you create or share
  • Location Data: Approximate location to show you nearby content
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app and its features
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Show you relevant content based on your location
  • Enable communication with other users
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With other users as part of the app's functionality
  • With service providers who help us operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transaction (merger, sale, etc.)

4. Your Choices

You can:

  • Update your account information in the app settings
  • Control who can see your content
  • Delete your account at any time
  • Opt out of location-based features

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Blips is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@blipsdigital.com
